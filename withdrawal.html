Withdrawal FAQ
Withdrawing Funds – How Does It Work?
At CryptoExchangerz, we have designed our withdrawal process
to be as easy as our funding process. To begin the withdrawal
process first select your preferred withdrawal method and then type in the amount you want to withdraw and click "Proceed".

What Methods Are There For Withdrawal Of Funds?
CryptoExchangerz provides three(3) withdrawal methods (Bitcoin, Paypal, and Direct Bank Transfer).

Must Withdrawal Requests Only Be Made At Certain Times?
Requests for withdrawals can be made at any time via the CryptoExchangerz site. The requests will be processed immediately, and during the relevant financial institution's business hours.

Is There A Withdrawal Limit?
Withdrawals are capped at the amount of funds that are currently in the account.

How Long Does It Take To Get My Money?
Withdrawal requests are addressed and handled as quickly as possible.
